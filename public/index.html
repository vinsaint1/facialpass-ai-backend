System Capabilities

Designed under object-oriented analysis principles and strict biometric security standards.

Face Detection Neural Net

Loads SSD MobileNetV1 neural network weights on the backend to detect human faces within milliseconds.

Landmarks Alignment

Extracts 68 landmark coordinates to map eyes, nose, and lips positions, compensating for head tilts and facial rotation.

128-Float Descriptor

Compiles biometric maps into secure 128-float mathematical vectors. No raw employee photos are stored in the database.

Euclidean Distance Classifier

Compares live face embeddings against stored records using high-dimensional Euclidean space matching with a strict 0.6 threshold.

Shift Schedule Manager

Enforces working shifts, tracking calendar slots, holidays, and active leaves to automatically mark logs compliance.

Absenteeism Risk Alerts

Features a predictive analytics engine that analyzes attendance frequencies to warn managers of high chronic absence risks.
